What is SmarterGiving?
SmarterGiving is a service used by a growing number of grantmakers and philanthropic sites to facilitate donations to nonprofits without having to deal with the tax and compliance complexity of doing so.
What does it cost?
Nothing. SmarterGiving is run by a group of nerds who got tired of seeing nonprofits get nickel and dimed by payment providers.
How is that possible?
It's a volunteer-run initiative with minimal overhead. Learn more .
